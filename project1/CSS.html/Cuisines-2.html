Indian Foods

Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani): Tender chicken in a mild, creamy tomato and butter sauce. Masala Dosa: A fermented rice and lentil crepe stuffed with spiced potatoes, served with coconut chutney.Rogan Josh: A fragrant, slow-cooked lamb curry originally from Kashmir. Sorshe Chingri: Bengali-style prawns steamed in a pungent and tangy mustard sauce. Dal Makhani: Slow-cooked black lentils and beans with butter and cream, a North Indian staple. Palak Paneer/Chole: Soft cottage cheese or chickpeas simmered in a spiced spinach gravy.
