Click here to visit CuisinesHub-UI Foodhub is an international online food ordering portal and technology company that connects consumers with local restaurants and takeaways. Competing with platforms like Just Eat, it uniquely utilizes a "fair price" 0% commission model for partner restaurants, allowing establishments to pass savings onto customers. Founders: Founded in 2008 by Ardian Mula and Mohammed Shakil. Headquarters: Stoke-on-Trent, England (with international operations). Services: Provides consumer food portals, EPOS (Electronic Point of Sale) systems, and delivery management for restaurants. Markets: Operates globally in the UK, Ireland, Australia, New Zealand, and the US.

The Business Model of Foodhub distinguishes itself in the highly competitive food delivery industry by not charging restaurants commission on orders. Instead of taking a cut of the restaurants' profits, the company charges business owners a flat software or tech fee. This enables takeaway partners to offer discounted prices (sometimes up to 30% cheaper than rival apps) directly to consumers on the Foodhub Portal.

What "Foodhub" Means: In the context of the company, a "Food Hub" refers to a digital marketplace rather than an agricultural distribution center. It acts as a central hub where customers can browse multiple takeaway menus, order food, and process payments through a single app or website.

Headquarters in India : DLF IT / ITES SEZ, 1st & 3rd Floor, Block 9A, Mount Poonamalle Road, Ramapuram, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India - 600089 Foodhub has expanded its footprint in Tamil Nadu, with regional branches operating in both Coimbatore and Madurai to support their rapidly growing employee base in India.
